Questions? We've Got You.

How is this different from regular shapewear?

Traditional shapewear applies uniform compression everywhere - that's why it feels like a torture device. Aura-Sculpt uses variable-tension zones: gentle at the chest so you can breathe, supportive at the waist for that held feeling, and lifting at the glutes without flattening. It's engineered to feel like a hug, not a squeeze.

What's the "anti-anxiety" aspect about?

The graduated compression mimics proprioceptive feedback - the same principle behind weighted blankets. This gentle, consistent pressure signals your parasympathetic nervous system to calm down. Many customers tell us they feel grounded and secure, not just "snatched."

What size should I order?

We recommend ordering your normal clothing size. The variable-tension zones adapt to your body. If you're between sizes, size up for a gentler hug or size down for more support. Our extended range goes from XXXS to 7X.

How does the magnetic closure work?

Micro-magnetic glide tracks replace bulky snaps. The panels seal automatically when they get close - no fumbling in dark bathrooms. The closure is thinner than paper, so you get zero visible lines even under bodycon dresses.

What's the return policy?

30-day no-questions-asked returns. If you don't feel that "held" sensation we promise, send it back. We'll refund everything including shipping. Your nervous system deserves to be happy.
